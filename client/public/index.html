About Our Society

Founded in 2010, the University Chess Society has grown into one of the most vibrant and successful chess communities in the collegiate circuit. We welcome players of all skill levels, from beginners learning their first moves to grandmasters perfecting their endgames.

Our mission is to promote the game of chess, develop strategic thinking skills, and build a supportive community where members can learn, compete, and grow together.

Through weekly club meetings, regular tournaments, and social events, we create an engaging environment that celebrates the rich tradition and exciting possibilities of chess.
